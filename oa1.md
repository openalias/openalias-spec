# OpenAlias v1 Specification

*Adopted 2014-2015*

> oa1:[prefix / asset_ticker] recipient_address=[address]; recipient_name=[text_description];

## OA1 Key-Value Pairs

* prefix / asset_ticker (required)
* recipient_address (required)
* recipient_name
* tx_description
* tx_amount
* tx_payment_id
* address_signature
* checksum
* "defining additional pairs is up to you"; "If you have a use-case where you feel certain key-value pairs may provide widespread use and benefit, please reach out to us and we can include it in the standard if appropriate."

## Official Prefixes

There is no official list of prefixes for OA1 at the time of writing.
